Why Partner With Us

Small budget. Serious governance.

We operate like an organisation ten times our size: published budgets, defined escalation protocols, independent monitoring, and alignment with Rwanda's national development strategy (NST2, Vision 2050) and the SDGs.

  • Institutionally vetted model with seed funding secured through a competitive selection
  • 100% of the budget published, with monitoring & evaluation costed from day one
  • A sustainability plan that outlives seed funding: sliding-scale fees subsidising free services
  • A replication blueprint any Rwandan district can adopt
