Traction & Recognition

A model already vetted by institutions

Selected as a winning venture — HATANA Innovation Bootcamp

In 2026, Ongera Ubeho was selected as a winning venture of the HATANA Innovation Bootcamp under the Social Entrepreneurship Fund 2.0 — an initiative of the Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program at the University of Rwanda. The selection followed a competitive review of the model's feasibility, community relevance, and social impact potential, and provides the seed funding for our six-month pilot.

Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program University of Rwanda Social Entrepreneurship Fund 2.0
